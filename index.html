Cyber Security

Objectives

The Union aims to enhance the protection of networks, information technology systems, operational technology systems and their components in terms of hardware and software, the services they provide, and the data they contain, taking into account the growing vital importance of cybersecurity in the lives of societies, and aims to establish a national industry in the field of cybersecurity that will achieve leadership in the Kingdom. In this regard, based on what is included in the vision of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia 2030.

The priorities of the Saudi Federation for Cybersecurity

  1. Attracting, qualifying and empowering qualified and ambitious national cadres.
  2. Building partnerships with public and private entities.
  3. Stimulating innovation and investment in cybersecurity.
  4. Contribute to achieving a technological renaissance that serves the future of the Kingdom's national economy.
